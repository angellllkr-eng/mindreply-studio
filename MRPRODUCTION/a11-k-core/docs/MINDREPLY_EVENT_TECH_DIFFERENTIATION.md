# MindReply Event-Tech Differentiation

## Positioning

> **Real-Time Event Tech, One Text Away.**

MindReply’s public commercial positioning is a focused, high-touch event-technology service for London clients. This is separate from A11-K’s engine-core identity.

## Differentiation

| Factor | MindReply direction | Typical competitor |
|---|---|---|
| Price | Custom / tailored pricing | Rigid packages or opaque enterprise quotes |
| Quality | Dedicated event-technology solutions | Mixed AV/IT services or generic software |
| Convenience | Direct, high-touch communication | Portals, ticket queues, or phone trees |
| Specialty | Focused event technology | Broad event production, IT consulting, or equipment rental |
| Proof | Early-stage public feedback footprint | Established review history |

## Strengths to develop

- **Frictionless direct communication:** a direct, approved support channel can be faster during live events than a ticket portal.
- **Pure event-tech focus:** lead with event apps, guest interaction, check-in, and communication outcomes rather than generic AV.
- **London agility:** local proximity can support fast venue, agency, and activation response.

## Improvements required

- Publish a clear service menu before expecting prospects to understand the offer.
- Add verified reviews/testimonials; never invent social proof.
- Confirm any public support number and service claims before placing them on public pages.

## A11-K relationship

MindReply remains the public commercial brand. Connected products may carry a quiet **Powered by A11-K** stamp, but A11-K must not replace MindReply’s voice.
