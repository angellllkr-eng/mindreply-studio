# UNAPOLAGETIC Brand System

Positioning:
Unapologetic style systems for people who move without asking permission.

Launch lane:
Fashion and accessories first. Cosmetics only after compliance approval.

Initial categories:
- statement accessories
- minimal streetwear
- beauty tools
- travel essentials
- self-care accessories
- creator lifestyle accessories

Blocked until review:
- regulated cosmetics
- ingestibles
- medical products
- counterfeit/replica products
- unsafe beauty claims
- fake luxury claims
